# Clarified Butter

### Ingredients

|Ingredients | US    |Metric |
|:-----------|:------|:------|
| Unsalted Butter | 1 lb | 454 g |

### Preparation

1. Melt butter in heavy saucepan **low heat**, *slowly* cook until the bubbling ceases and liquid turns clear **30 to 40 minutes.**

2. Strain and cool, be sure to leave any solids in the bottom of the pan.
3. Store in an airtight container in the fridge for up to a month.
